Scrambled Eggs

Scrambled eggs are a simple breaking dish made with just eggs and butter,and can be prepared in under five minutes.
Thetexture is soft and creamy beccause they are cooked over low heat and stirred gently.
this dish is perfwct for days when you want something light,quick,and easy to make.

Ingredients

  • 2-3 eggs
  • 1 tablespoon butter
  • A little salt and pepper
  • 1-2 tablespoons of mlik

Instructions

  1. Beat the eggs and mix in a small amount of salt.
  2. Heat a pan over low heat and melt the butter.
  3. Pour in the eggs and stir gently until they begin to set , then turn off the heat.
  4. Sprinkle with pepper and serve.
